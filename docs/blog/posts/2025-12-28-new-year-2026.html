    Cool Stuff From 2025

    on a lighter note, here's some of my favorite things that came out/happened this year!
    hover/tap on the pictures for my thoughts/info on each thing.

    Hollow Knight: Silksong
    feels obvious this would be here lol but yeah this game absolutely lived up to my expectations. it expands on the first game in so many ways and is every bit as challenging and fun.

    Momentum Mod
    Momentum Mod opened up its playtest for everyone this year! its a really neat standalone game that centralizes source game movement modes (bhop, surf, rocket jump, etc.) into one place. if you've ever been interested in these modes, i think this is easily the best place to get into it.

    BAIONLENJA
    this game rules. it takes inspiration from source movement but makes it much simpler to execute on. the open level design and uncapped speed let you zoom around yet it still takes some precision to get through. the game is in early access, but what's there makes me super excited to see what the dev has planned!

    DELTARUNE Chapters 3+4
    kinda wild to think that silksong and more deltarune got released in the same year. these two alone would make a great year for games lol. anyway yep it's more deltarune, i don't have much to say other than ofc it's really good. i've especially been enjoying this game's variation of battles compared to undertale.

    Mosa Lina (Second Layer)
    Mosa Lina got an update this year that basically doubled the amount of content in the game. i hadn't played the game before this year, but man this game is fun to play around in. it's a platformer that will constantly make you feel clever and stupid in the span of minutes. and for how replayable and deep this game's mechanics are, it's a shame it doesn't get more attention. it's also absurdly cheap!
