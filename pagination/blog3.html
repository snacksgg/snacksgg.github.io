Outside of art I've been playing a handful of games. Heres some quick thoughts:

click to open thoughtsPseudoregalia - The freedom of movement in this game is amazing, one of the most fun games to just move around in that I've played. Wonderful soundtrack, art direction, atmosphere, and platform challenges. My only complaint is that one key movement ability seemed to be gated behind obscure platforming, which I ended up getting stuck on. A little frustrating but still a great experience.
Pepper Grinder - Really interesting 2d platformer, another one where traversing the levels is fun by itself. The pixel art style and environments look great, and the levels have a nice flow to them. Bosses were lackluster but still fine for a platformer.
Penny's Big Breakaway - I have some mixed feelings about this one, but I still think it's a great game and recommend it if it looks fun to you. Imo, this game becomes a lot more fun when you ignore the collectables and just go for combos/fast times. This was hard for me to do on a first playthrough since they unlock bonus levels. Otherwise the movement system is a lot of fun once you learn it and the whole game was a pleasure from start to end.

I just realized after writing all that they all start with P, huh.
