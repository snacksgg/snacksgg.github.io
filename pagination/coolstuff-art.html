  • Interference

    Drawing Telephone game with a longstanding and friendly community. A fun diversion when you don't know what to draw or want to warm up! I would also recommend Drawception for something similar but the site has been neglected for a while.

    A collaborative comic-making website. A fan recreation of PanelJam.

Art Misc.

    • Podcasts •
  • Creative Block

    An art focused podcast where creators are interviewed while doodling together. A ton of great interviews with both industry and independent artists!
    Some of my favorite episodes: Worthikids, Tyson Hesse, Jhonen Vasquez, Louie Zong, Jorge Guitierrez, Yotam Perel, Jeff Rosenstock

    • • Programs •
  • Allusion

    Allusion is a program that lets you organize images. Its got filters, tags, sorting, and it's free! I use this program to gather inspiration and references, it's super easy to tag and organize.
    Another good reference manager: PureRef
