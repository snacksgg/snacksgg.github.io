DoigSwift

Type: Artist (Stream)

Doig and Swift have a really unique stream where they control characters in a custom 3D environment while working on art. Their characters are so expressive and fun, they remind me of LittleBigPlanet characters. Always a cozy watch!

Allusion

Type: Program - Free

Allusion has everything I could want from a reference library. Its got filters, tags, sorting, and it's free! I use this program to gather inspiration and references, it's super easy to tag and organize images.
Another good reference manager: PureRef

Interference

Type: Website (Art Game)

Drawing Telephone game with with a longstanding and friendly community. A fun diversion when you don't know what to draw or want to warm up! I would also recommend Drawception for something similar but the site has been neglected for a while.

PanelChutney

Type: Website (Art Game)

A collaborative comic-making website. A fan recreation of PanelJam.

Creative Block

Type: Podcast

An art focused podcast where creators are interviewed while doodling together. A ton of great interviews with both industry and independent artists!
Some of my favorite episodes: Worthikids, Tyson Hesse, Jhonen Vasquez, Louie Zong, Jorge Guitierrez, Yotam Perel, Jeff Rosenstock
