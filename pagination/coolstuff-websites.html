Whirled Club

Type: Virtual World

Whirled was a virtual world where you could chat, play Flash games, make rooms and more. The majority of stuff in the game was user-made. Avatars, rooms, pets, furniture, games are all made by users. This is a restoration of the site. Since Flash is dead you'll have to download a client to play. I used to go on Whirled all the time as a kid so there's a lot of nostalgia for me here.

deepcut.fm

Type: Music Sharing/DJing

A music sharing site where users "DJ" by queueing songs in a lobby. This site has been around for a while, previously known as turntable.fm. Users create rooms that usually have a theme or genre. A unique way to share and discover music!
